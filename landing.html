Inside the Funnel

Every Page in the Funnel Has a Job

Instead of random pages stitched together, Sales Funnel Pro gives each page a specific role so your visitors always know what to do next.

1. Landing Page (Attention & Desire)

Grabs attention with a clear promise, tells the story behind your offer, and uses social proof and proof points to move visitors from curious to excited.

2. Checkout Page (Decision & Trust)

Makes the purchase decision feel safe and easy with a clean layout, benefit bullets, guarantee section, and minimal distractions around the payment form.

3. Upsell Page (Maximizing Revenue)

Presents a natural, higher‑value next step with a one‑click add‑on option so you can dramatically lift average order value without adding more traffic.

4. Downsell Page (Saving the Sale)

Catches customers who say “no” to the upsell and offers a lighter, more affordable option instead of losing them entirely, turning more visitors into buyers.

5. Thank‑You Page (Retention & Referrals)

Confirms the purchase, sets expectations, and introduces next steps, including referral prompts, onboarding, or simple follow‑up offers.

6. Bonus / Content Pages (Value & Nurture)

Optional pages for delivering free trainings, checklists, or case studies that nurture your audience and warm them up for future offers.
